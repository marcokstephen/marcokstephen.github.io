BBC Radio with time zone delays

This site streams BBC Radio 1, Radio 2, and Radio 4 with hourly delays to give the illusion of real-time listening regardless of your time zone. Currently it supports hourly time zones (sorry Newfoundland) ranging from Britain minus one hour, to Britain minus eight hours. Sorry, no time zones ahead of Britain. :(

Time zone guide

This guide is off by an hour for half a month in March and half a month in November, given the different dates for daylight savings between Britain and North America.
  • Atlantic: Britain - 4 hours
  • Eastern: Britain - 5 hours
  • Central: Britain - 6 hours
  • Mountain: Britain - 7 hours
  • Pacific: Britain - 8 hours

How do I use this?

If you are on a desktop computer you can simply click the stream and it should work in your browser. If you are on mobile, you may need to open the stream in an app like VLC. Copy the link of the stream you want, find an option to "Open Network Stream" within your desired app, and paste the link of the stream there.
